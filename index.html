What is it?

Ourea is a package manager for non-jailbroken devices. With more and more awesome shortcuts than ever appearing on the market, this is a centralized place to get them all.

Downloads:

Installing Ourea is very easy. Just press the "Download .ipa" button and install it through your method of choice. Do note that, just like any other sideloaded app, it will need to be refreshed every once in a while.
